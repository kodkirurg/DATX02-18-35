TITLE Inventory Use

DESCRIPTION START
Here we need to learn how to use the inventory. To open the inventory either press the briefcase at the toolbar at the top or press the left border of the screen.

Once in the inventory you may introduce another card to the game board by selecting it.

Then close the inventory to get back to the game board, by either clicking the briefcase or on the right border of the screen.
DESCRIPTION END
