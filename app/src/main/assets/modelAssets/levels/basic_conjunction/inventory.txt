TITLE Inventory Use

DESCRIPTION START
Here we need to learn how to use the inventory to complete the level. To open the inventory either press the briefcase at the toolbar at the top or press the left border of the screen.

Once you are in the inventory you may introduce another card to the game board by clicking on it. Then close the inventory again by clicking the briefcase again or on the right border of the screen.
