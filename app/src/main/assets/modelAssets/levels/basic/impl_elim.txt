TITLE Implication Elimination

DESCRIPTION START
Here we introduce a new kind of card, the implication card.

The implication card can be combined with whatever card is over/to the left of the implication arrow to produce the card to the right of the implication arrow.

Go ahead and give it a try by selecting the implication card and then the card with the Sun.
DESCRIPTION END
