TITLE Basic Implication

DESCRIPTION START
Here we introduce a new kind of card, the implication card.
The implication card can be combined with whatever card is to the left of the implication arrow to produce the card to the right of the implication arrow
Go ahead and give it a try by first selecting the implication card and then the card with the blue ball.
